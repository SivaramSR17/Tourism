Top Attractions

Vivekananda Rock Memorial

A sacred monument built in honor of Swami Vivekananda, located on a small island. It is known for its spiritual significance and panoramic ocean views.

View on Map

Thiruvalluvar Statue

A majestic 133-foot tall statue dedicated to the ancient Tamil poet Thiruvalluvar. It stands beside the Vivekananda Rock Memorial and represents Tamil culture and literature.

View on Map

Kanyakumari Beach

Famous for the meeting point of three oceans and breathtaking sunrise and sunset views, Kanyakumari Beach offers a unique natural experience.

View on Map

Padmanabhapuram Palace

A beautifully preserved wooden palace complex from the 16th century, showcasing traditional Kerala architecture and royal artifacts.

View on Map

Suchindram Temple

A famous Hindu temple known for its unique architecture, musical pillars, and deities that combine the trinity of Hinduism: Shiva, Vishnu, and Brahma.
